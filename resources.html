STEPH'S RECS is coprised of many interest categories, all handpicked by Steph. She is constantly inspired by aromas, food markets and various online resources. Read on to understand how she's organized her addresses, what she reads and who has provided the beautiful photos on the site.

Here's a breakdown. You have the choice from the navigation bar to Choose Your Destination.

You have the option of selecting Africa, Asia, The Caribbean, Central America, Europe, The Middle East, Oceania, South America and North America.

Within each Destination you will have the choice of a variety of countries, ranging from The USA, Mexico, France and even Morocco!

Once you select a Country you will have a list of addresses with Categories such as Eat & Drink, Stay, Activity each with sub categories which will help narrow down your search. You can find Museums and Art Galleries, Shopping, places to Eat and places to Drink. The site has a strong offering in Cuisine and Libations which is why you will have numerous category options, surely sufficient to satisfy your tastebuds!
