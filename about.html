About

STEPH'S RECS is a personal and unique compliation of addresses gathered from years of travel and experiences around the globe. The idea was inspired by frustrations when searching for a restaurant or hotel on applications and websites geared toward travel. I remarked that having to click on individual dots on a map, each representing a location, was time-consuming and not particularly user-friendly. I desired to have an orgaized list of my own addresses, which can be easily filtered by specific interest categories.

This desire to streamline the search for family and friends when they ask for recommendations, but also for myself when I am searching for where to eat or an activity to do with friends, served as the inspiration for STEPH'S RECS.
